College Recommendation System

“College Recommendation system” is a type of recommendation system, which generally recommends college according to the user’s query. This project is an attempt to help people by giving them proper information about the college where their related education problems are solved. People face different types of problem regarding college. They seek for college so that they can get proper education, but do not get proper information about where to visit and find the college. Searching college information and choosing it, takes more time. There may not be the proper information about colleges Therefore, to eliminate this type of confusions of choosing college our system helps in an easy way where they can get their solution easily.
